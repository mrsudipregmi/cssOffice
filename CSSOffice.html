Office Solutions for

Safe and Healthy Workspaces 

As businesses begin to bring workers back to the office, changes may be required to create safe post COVID19 workspaces. Our CSS Team is here to help you make the transition back into the office easy and increase health safety. 

Contact our team for a no-cost, no-obligation site consultation and 
learn how we can help your team return to a safe working environment.

If you cant keep up on the lists of requirements from Alberta Health Services and don't know where to start, have no fear! Our team is always current on the latest updates and will design and configure a post COVID office space that meets your needs. 

From custom safety shields to protect the reception area to updated workstation layouts that will allow your team to maintain social distancing when you return to the office, CSS is here to help you navigate the post COVID19 office design.
